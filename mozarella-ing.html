FRIED MOZARELLA
CHEESE STICKS

Easy Cook Recipe

Ingredients
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 1/2 cups Italian seasoned bread crumps
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic salt
  • 2/3 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/3 cup cornstarch
  • 2 cups oil for frying , or as needed
  • 1 (16 ounce) package mozzarella cheese sticks
    Procedures
  • 1

    Gather all the ingredients

  • 2

    Whisk water and eggs together in a small bowl. Mix bread crumbs and garlic salt together in a medium bowl. Blend flour and cornstarch together in a third bowl.

  • 3

    Heat oil to 365 degrees F (185 degrees C) in a large, heavy saucepan.

  • 4

    Dredge a mozzarella stick in flour; shake off excess. Dip into egg mixture. Lift up so excess egg drips back in the bowl. Press into bread crumbs to coat. Place breaded mozzarella stick on a plate or wire rack. Repeat with remaining mozzarella sticks.


  • 5

    Use a spider spoon or a pair of tongs to lower 3 to 4 mozzarella sticks into the hot oil. Fry until golden brown, about 30 seconds. Remove from heat and drain on paper towels. Repeat to fry remaining mozzarella sticks.

  • 6

    Serve and Enjoy!

Additional Notes
How to Store, Freeze and Reheat Mozzarella Sticks

Store (completely cooled) homemade mozzarella sticks in an airtight container in the fridge for up to five days. Line the container with paper towels to absorb excess oil.


You can freeze mozzarella sticks. Allow them to cool completely, arrange them in a single layer on a baking sheet, and freeze for a few hours or overnight. Once the sticks are frozen, transfer them to a zip-top bag labeled with the date. Freeze for up to three months.

Reheat refrigerated or frozen mozzarella sticks in the oven. To reheat refrigerated cheese sticks, place them in an oven preheated to 350 degrees F for about 5 minutes, or until heated through. To reheat frozen cheese sticks, place them in an oven preheated to 450 degrees F for 10-15 minutes, or until heated through.
